About

This website was created as part of the course “Organization and Digital Methods in the Cultural Heritage Domain” at the University of Bologna (2025).

The project explores the life and legacy of Hedy Lamarr, combining cinematic history and her groundbreaking inventions. It showcases curated resources—from films and photographs to technical patents—semantically described with linked data standards.

Team

The website was developed by students from the Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge Master’s program at the University of Bologna, with academic supervision by Professor Francesca Tomasi..
